RÓLUNK

After my parents have died from hunger, while waiting for the pizza delivery guy, I’ve decided to never let anybody else suffer the same fate as I did. I created a pizza company that whole mission is to bring delicious pizza to anybody at lightning speed.

After realizing that he can never taste pizza again, due to all the onion in it, Raul decided that if he can’t eat pizza, than he will just make pizza instead. He might never eat pizza again, but the smiles on the faces he makes pizza for makes up for it for him.
